About us

Hangzhou Hunting Board Network Technology Co., Ltd. is an intelligent factory headquartered in Wanda Commercial Center, Hangzhou, China, that focus on PCB rapid-prototyping. We have a modern workplace of 10.76K sq.ft in Hangzhou, and established our production base that covers an area of 6.92 acre in Zhuhai, Guangdong, China.

Applying Industry 4.0 technology and connecting online and offline channels, we create a new intelligentialized platform for PCB fabrication, and change the traditional transaction mode of PCB.

Concentrating our efforts in fields of automotive electronics, instrumentation, intelligent equipment, industrial automation, and the Internet of Things, we use modern technology to provide our customers with customization and prototyping service of 1~8-layer PCB boards within 24 hours.
